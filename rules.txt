SmartCup League Tournament Rules
How Match Predictions and the Season Leaderboard Work
SmartCup League is a decentralized football prediction game built around two simultaneous competitions:
Match-by-Match Predictions — earn rewards after each match.
Season-Long Leaderboard — accumulate points across the entire tournament and compete for the Final Prize Pool.
Every prediction participates in both games at the same time.
1. Match Predictions (Match-by-Match Game)
The Pari-Mutuel Pool System
SmartCup League uses an Automated Market Mechanism (AMM) based on a pari-mutuel pool.
This means:
There are no fixed odds
There is no bookmaker or house
Payouts depend entirely on player participation
All predictions for a match are collected into a shared pool, and winners split that pool proportionally once the match result is confirmed.
How the Match Pool Is Distributed
For every prediction placed:
75% goes to the Match Winner Pool
20% goes to the Season-Long Final Prize Pool
5% goes to the DAO treasury as a protocol fee
Only the 75% match pool is distributed among winners of that specific match.
Why Payouts Are Variable
The payout is calculated as:
Match Pool ÷ Number of Winning Predictions
This means:
Rare correct predictions pay more
Popular outcomes pay less
A correct prediction may sometimes return less than the entry amount if many users predicted the same result
This is a natural outcome of the shared pari-mutuel pool system.

Example
100 players enter a match:
Total entries: 300 USDC
Match pool (75%): 225 USDC
Scenario A — 10 winners
Each winner receives 22.5 USDC
Scenario B — 150 winners
Each winner receives 1.5 USDC
Both predictions are correct — the difference is how many players share the pool.

Prediction Rules
For each match:
Minimum prediction entry: 3 USDC
Only one prediction per wallet per match
Predictions close 10 minutes before kickoff
Predictions cannot be edited or cancelled after submission
These rules are enforced automatically by smart contracts.


2. What You Can Predict
Players submit one score prediction per match.
Your score determines both points and payout eligibility.
Group Stage Matches
Exact score prediction
→ 3 points + eligible for payout
Correct outcome (win / draw / loss)
→ 1 point + eligible for payout
Incorrect prediction
→ 0 points

Knockout Matches
Players predict the full-time score.
If predicting a draw, they must also select the penalty winner.
If the match ends in a draw:
The penalty winner determines the final outcome
Correct winner after penalties earns points

3. Match Settlement and Rewards
After the match ends:
The match result is confirmed by an oracle
Smart contracts verify the result
The match pool is distributed automatically
Winning players can claim their rewards directly from the smart contract.
Key rules:
Winners share 75% of the pool
Rewards are distributed proportionally
If no one wins, the match pool is added to the Final Prize Pool
All settlements are executed fully on-chain and cannot be modified by any operator.

4. The Season-Long Leaderboard
Every prediction also contributes to a global tournament leaderboard.
Points accumulate throughout the tournament, and players compete to reach the Top 5 positions.
This leaderboard creates a season-long competition across all matches.

20% of every match entry
Unclaimed or unused match pool balances
After the tournament ends:

Top players claim rewards on-chain

Tie-Break Rule
If two or more players finish the tournament with the same number of points, the prize allocations for the tied positions are combined and divided equally among them.
Examples:
If two players tie for 1st place, the rewards for 1st and 2nd place (40% + 25%) are combined and split equally between them. Each player receives 32.5% of the Final Prize Pool.


If three players tie for 2nd place, the rewards for 2nd, 3rd, and 4th place (25% + 20% + 10%) are combined and divided equally among the three players.


This rule ensures fair distribution whenever players finish with the same leaderboard score.

6. Key Principles of SmartCup League
SmartCup League is designed to be:
Non-custodial
Players always control their own wallets.
House-free
The protocol never sets odds or takes the opposite side of predictions.
Transparent
All pools, payouts, and leaderboard rankings are verifiable on-chain.
Fully automated
Smart contracts enforce all rules, including prediction timing, payouts, and leaderboard updates.
